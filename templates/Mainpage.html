Why this app

This tool is made for doctors everywhere to get quick up to date information on recent publications needed for the diagnosis of patients. Variants in genes of which the gene's - clinical picture / disease-characteristic associations have recently been published (Pubmed) may be missed in genome diagnostics with gene panel analyzes. This could cause misdiagnosis here for this tool to prevent doctors from making this mistake

About this app

Web application for identifying recently published literature association (s) of genes with disease characteristics (disease characteristics depending on selected gene panel or patient disease characteristics, starting with eg intellectual disability). Based on this, interesting variants can be searched for possible new gene - disease / disease characteristics associations which can also be used for updating gene panels. The result can be an overview of genes (HGNC symbol) and recently published articles (Pubmed references) in which an association was found with 1 or more of the entered clinical characteristics (which ?, and possibly additional clinical characteristics from the articles and e.g. inheritance form). Optionally, information from other data sources is also added in order to make a good assessment of whether a gene is a new disease gene and whether known clinical core traits match the entered clinical core traits.
